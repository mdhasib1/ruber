Contract Rules and Terms

1. Your Contract with Us

By paying for the rental in this contract, you agree to all terms and conditions within it. Please read it carefully. Contact a staff member for clarification if needed.

2. Rental Period

The vehicle is available for the period specified in the contract. Late returns incur a penalty of 30 CHF per hour, up to a maximum of 150 CHF. Insurance coverage lapses if returned after the agreed time.

3. Customer Responsibility

The customer is liable for any damages and costs to restore the vehicle to its original condition. Unauthorized repairs are prohibited, and immediate notification is required for any incident or theft.

4. Company Responsibility

Existing vehicle damages are noted before contract signing. The vehicle has been maintained per manufacturer standards and is road-ready at rental start.

5. Ownership and Personal Belongings

The customer is responsible for removing personal items at rental end. The company is not liable for any items left behind.

6. Vehicle Usage Conditions

Only authorized drivers can operate the vehicle. It must not be rented out or used for illegal activities, off-road driving, or without insurance coverage.

7. Pricing

Agreed prices are exempt from VAT. The customer is liable for rental fees, damage costs, fuel, legal fees, and any fines incurred during the rental period.

8. Insurance

Insurance terms are accepted upon contract signing. The deductible is the customer's responsibility unless otherwise noted.

9. Accident or Theft Procedures

In the event of an accident, avoid admitting fault and document all details for the police and company. For theft, promptly report and confirm the incident in writing.

10. Data Protection

By signing, you consent to the processing and storage of your personal data related to this contract. We use your data for analysis, credit control, and asset protection.

11. Contract Termination

The contract may be terminated if you breach significant terms. Termination does not affect any outstanding obligations under the contract.

12. Governing Law

This contract is governed by Swiss law. Disputes will be handled fairly and, if unresolved, may proceed to a Swiss court in Lugano.
